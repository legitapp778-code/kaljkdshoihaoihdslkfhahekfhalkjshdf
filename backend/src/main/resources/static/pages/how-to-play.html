Step-by-Step Guide

1

Place Your Bet

Select your desired bet amount using the chips or manual input. Then, click on "BET ON TOTA" or "BET ON MENA".

2

Wait for the Spin

Once the round timer finishes, the game board will randomly select a winning bird for each row.

3

Collect Winnings

If your predicted bird appears on the winning row, your bet is multiplied! The standard multiplier is 2.00x, instantly credited to your wallet.

Pro Tips

Martingale Strategy

Double your bet after every loss. When you win, you recover all previous losses plus your original profit. Be careful of table limits!

Glossary

Multiplier

The factor by which your bet is multiplied if you win.

Round ID

A unique identifier for every single game played.

Bankroll

The total amount of money in your wallet available to bet.

RNG

Random Number Generator. Ensures every outcome is fair.
