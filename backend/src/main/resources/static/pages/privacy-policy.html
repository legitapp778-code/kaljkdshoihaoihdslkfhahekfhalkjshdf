Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We collect information to provide a secure and compliant gaming environment. This includes your name, date of birth, contact details, payment information, and device data (IP address, browser type).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for account verification, processing transactions, preventing fraud, and complying with legal and regulatory obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect your sensitive data during transmission. All stored data is protected by advanced firewall systems.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Contact our Data Protection Officer for any privacy-related requests.
